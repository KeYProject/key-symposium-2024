Payment
Deadline: July, 7th.
This year, we are working with registration fees instead of posterior invoices. We are planning to other the following booking options:
  1. Regular: (ca. 520 EUR) includes everything: The overnight stay (single bedroom) at "Haus der Kirche", the conference costs (breakfast, lunch, dinner), the social dinner.
  2. Regular w/o overnight stay (ca. 320 EUR): All of regular but we do not book a room for you. This might be a choice. If you want to come with family and you stay at a different hotel. "Haus der Kirche" only offers double bedrooms.
  3. Dinner-only (59 EUR): To enable the participation of our alumni at the social event, we will offer theoption of joining us at the social event.

Deadline to announce : July, 7th.

You can register for as many talks/discussions as you like using the form below.

Until July 7th, you should at least announce your talks with a minimal version: ("Title: tbd" and some keywords in the abstract to find a suitable session).

But we would appreciate even an early announcement. The schedule is tight, so please announce in time. Your entry should be properly finalized until July, 21st with a proper title and abstract so that we can compile the final schedule.

Note the novelty: You can select one of three tracks. As the KeYmaera group joined our symposium, we will split some sessions. Your choices are the common track (KeY+KeYmaera) or KeY/KeYmaera- specific track. The final decision is made by the program board (Enguerrand Prebet and Alexander Weigl).
